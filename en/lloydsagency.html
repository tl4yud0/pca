We are Lloyd's

Agency

Our office in Guadalajara was appointed in 2006 as Lloyd’s Agent by Lloyd’s of London. This appointment is only given to companies with proven ethical and professional standards. The quality on the performance of any Lloyd’s Agent is guaranteed worldwide. We are convinced that there is no other international body of surveyors that can meet our professionalism and expertise.

Being part of the worldwide Lloyd’s Agency Network provides us access to more than 350 surveyors around the world, covering practically any geographical location in the globe. We hope part of this worldwide presence can be of help to our customers.
