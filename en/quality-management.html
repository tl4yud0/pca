Scope of the quality management system

PCA Group. in a constant search for excellence in its processes and quality of its services, under ISO 9001: 2015 standards

It is an International Standard that establishes the requirements for the implementation of a Quality Management System for an organization that provides products or services, in order to meet the needstates and / or expectations of customers, as well as the legal requirements that apply. It is awarded by the International Organization for Standardization.

PCA Group has a staff with huge experience that allows providing the highest quality and optimal Adjustment and Inspection services, adopting a process-based approach with the development, implementation and continuous improvement of a Quality Management System.

The scope of the SGC includes the operational, quality, commercial, suppliers, systems, human and administrative capital of the corporate of the damage adjustment services of the organization for the insurance sector, in accordance with the local legal framework in the following categories marked at the CNSF:

Type AJ-III: Freight Transportation.

Type AJ-V: Other Damage Hazards
